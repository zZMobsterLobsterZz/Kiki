About

Kiki's Earth Quest is a gamified solution to smart and sustainable cities by targeting schools and children. The app packages SDGs into bite-sized, real-life challenges for kids. Users can create communities, where they compete against each other to become the most sustainable citizen.


The game addresses two objectives by focusing on two groups of interest.



Adults

To facilitate the transfer of knowledge between the city and its citizens through interactive community learning



Kids

To cultivate a positive and empowered attitude towards SDGs from a young age
